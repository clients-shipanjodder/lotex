Design, Construction

Design, Construction

Design, construction and construction – If you want to build or change your existing living or business space, LOTEX GROUP provides you with design services with execution to the very end.

We also provide design, construction and construction services for industrial buildings, residential and public buildings.

In order to reach the ultimate goal, our team works on creating a project solution with a 3D visual model, making a technical drawing of the project, developing the practicality and functionality of the space, selecting and defining materials, colors, decorations and all other details.

Production of steel structures

Production of steel structures

We are especially proud of the opportunity to meet a wide range of customer needs in the field of steel structures.

The use of the most modern world technologies in this area of the construction industry allows us to offer the most favorable conditions for the procurement of pipes of large diameters and thicknesses, cold-formed profiles, sheet metal cutting with gas, plasma or laser, robotic welding, corrosion protection and numerous other services and materials.

Consulting services

Consulting services

Consulting services and performing professional technical supervision on the construction and reconstruction of all types of facilities.

Our team consists of graduate civil engineers (licenses ICS 410 and 310), graduate engineers of architecture (licenses ICS 400 and 300), graduate economists, graduate political scientist for international affairs, architectural technicians, electrical technicians, reinforcement workers, carpenters, masons.
